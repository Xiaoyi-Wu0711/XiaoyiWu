Reinforcement Learning

Unlike supervised and unsupervised learning, reinforcement learning (RL) simulates agent interactions based on a series of action-reward mechanisms. Thus, it mimics human's decision-making process.
As a research assistant at Collective Decision Intelligence Lab at Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA) from 2020, I mastered the principles and techniques of RL and deep learning.

  • Jidi Platform Development

    Jidi is a Kaggle-like platform that provides users with diverse environments, high-quality competition, real-time discussions and fair algorithm rankings. I programmed simulation environments and baseline algorithms for the platform.

  • Multi-task Coalitional Game

    Based on centralized training and decentralized execution paradigm, this research focuses on team structure optimization and resource allocation in multi-task coordinated planning.
